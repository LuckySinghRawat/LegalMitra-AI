## 🔗 HOW THIS DATA IS USED

### In the Application:

```javascript
1. User submits complaint (e.g., bought defective phone)
   ↓
2. AI Analysis reads: "I bought defective phone, want refund"
   ↓
3. AI matches to Category: "Consumer"
   ↓
4. System looks in indianLaws.json["Consumer"]
   ↓
5. Returns all 5 Consumer laws:
   - Consumer Protection Act 2019 - Section 2(7)
   - Consumer Protection Act 2019 - Section 35
   - Consumer Protection Act 2019 - Section 39
   - Sale of Goods Act 1930 - Section 16
   - E-Commerce Rules 2020 - Rule 4
   ↓
6. Display to user with descriptions
```

## 📌 KEY TAKEAWAY

**indianLaws.json** is the **knowledge base** that helps the AI system:
1. ✅ Understand which laws apply to different types of complaints
2. ✅ Provide accurate legal section references
3. ✅ Suggest appropriate authorities to approach
4. ✅ Generate formal complaint letters with legal citations
5. ✅ Enable complainants to understand their legal standing

Without this dataset, the AI would rely only on general knowledge and couldn't provide precise Indian legal references!
